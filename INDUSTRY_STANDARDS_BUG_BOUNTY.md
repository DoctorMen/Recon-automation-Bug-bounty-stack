## Industry-Documented Report Quality Criteria

### HackerOne Quality Report Standards

**Required Components:**
1. Clear and concise title
2. Detailed reproduction steps
3. Proof of concept (PoC)
4. Impact assessment
5. Remediation advice

**Source**: docs.hackerone.com/articles/8475116-quality-reports

### Bugcrowd & Intigriti Standards

**Report Quality Factors:**
1. Step-by-step reproduction instructions
2. URLs, parameters, and payloads included
3. Evidence (screenshots, videos, code snippets)
4. Impact evaluation (data exposure, system compromise)
5. Remediation suggestions

**Source**: intigriti.com/researchers/blog
