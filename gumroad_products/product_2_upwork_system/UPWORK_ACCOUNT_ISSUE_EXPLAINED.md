## üéØ WHAT THIS MEANS

### **The Issue:**
Your Upwork account doesn't have permission to **sell** projects in the Project Catalog feature.

**Project Catalog** is Upwork's marketplace where freelancers can create pre-priced services that clients can buy directly (like your security scan listing).

---

## üîç WHY THIS HAPPENS

### **Common Reasons:**

1. **Account Type Mismatch**
   - You might be logged in as a **client account** instead of **freelancer account**
   - Project Catalog requires a freelancer profile

2. **Account Not Eligible**
   - New accounts sometimes need approval
   - Account might need verification
   - Profile might not be complete enough

3. **Feature Not Available**
   - Project Catalog might not be enabled for your account
   - Might need to request access
   - Could be location/account type restriction

4. **Account Restrictions**
   - Account might have restrictions
   - Previous policy violations
   - Payment/suspension issues

---

## üîß HOW TO FIX IT

### **Step 1: Check Account Type**
- Make sure you're logged in as a **freelancer** (not client)
- Check your profile type in Upwork settings

### **Step 2: Verify Profile Completeness**
- Complete your freelancer profile 100%
- Add all required information
- Upload portfolio samples
- Add skills and certifications

### **Step 3: Request Project Catalog Access**
- Contact Upwork Support
- Ask: "How do I enable Project Catalog for my freelancer account?"
- Explain you want to create a service listing

### **Step 4: Check Account Status**
- Verify account is in good standing
- Check for any restrictions or warnings
- Ensure payment method is verified

---

## üéØ ALTERNATIVE: REGULAR JOB POSTINGS

**This still works** - you'll just be responding to job posts instead of having a catalog listing.
