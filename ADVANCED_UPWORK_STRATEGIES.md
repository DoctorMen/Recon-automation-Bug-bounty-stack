**Bronze ($200):**
- Basic scan
- Simple report
- Email support

**Silver ($400):**
- Comprehensive scan
- Professional report
- Phone support
- 30-day follow-up

**Gold ($800):**
- Full assessment
- Executive + technical reports
- Priority support
- Remediation guidance
- Monthly monitoring offer

**Psychology:** Most choose middle tier (Silver)

**Message Structure:**

2. "But I can do a focused scan for $500"

**Result:** $200 feels like deal (vs $1,500 anchor)

Bundle services together:

**Individual Pricing:**
- Security scan: $300
- Monthly monitoring: $500/month
- Total: $800

**Package Pricing:**
- Scan + 3 months monitoring: $1,200
- Savings: $600 (25% discount)
- Client feels they're getting deal
