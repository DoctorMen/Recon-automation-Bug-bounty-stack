## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### **Tip 1: Let the Demo Breathe**
Don't narrate every second. Run the full pipeline, then **be quiet** for 5 seconds. Let them absorb. Silence is powerful.

### **Tip 2: Use Their Language**
If they say "bandwidth," say "bandwidth."  
If they say "capacity," say "capacity."  
Mirror their terminology.

### **Tip 3: Anchor on ROI**
"$297/month is less than one billable hour for most consultants, but it gives you 40x execution speed."

### **Tip 4: Show, Don't Tell**
Instead of "it's fast," click the button and let them see 20-second execution.  
Instead of "it's easy," click "Vibe Command" and show natural language.

### **Tip 5: Handle Technical Glitches Gracefully**
If something breaks: "That's actually perfect - this is why we have error handling. Let me show you the fallback..."  
Turn bugs into features.
