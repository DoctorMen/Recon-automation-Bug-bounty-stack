## üéØ PROJECT CATALOG REQUIREMENTS

Based on Upwork's Terms of Service and policies:

### **Eligibility Requirements:**

1. **Account Type:**
   - Must be a **freelancer account** (not client account)
   - Cannot use Project Catalog with client-only accounts

2. **Profile Completion:**
   - Profile must be **100% complete**
   - Required: Photo, headline, overview, skills, portfolio
   - Payment method must be verified

3. **Account Standing:**
   - Account must be in **good standing**
   - No policy violations
   - No payment issues
   - No restrictions or suspensions

4. **Earnings History:**
   - Some sources indicate **$1,000+ earnings** may be required
   - OR account must be **Top Rated** or **Rising Talent**
   - OR account must have **JSS (Job Success Score)** of 80%+

5. **Verification:**
   - Identity verification may be required
   - Phone verification
   - Payment method verification

6. **Location/Restrictions:**
   - Project Catalog may not be available in all countries
   - Some regions restricted from Project Catalog
